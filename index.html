About Us

Building the future through applied research.

We are a research-based organization dedicated to developing innovative end to end solutions for a wide range of problems in society and industry. The company has been formed under Faculty Entrepreneurship Policy, and IIT Kanpur holds 10% equity. We are a team of highly skilled scientists, researchers, engineers, and analysts who work continuously to push the boundaries of technology and to provide the service and products that meet the needs of clients. All the persons who are involved in the organization are also the stakeholders.

Mission

To learn from each challenge and continuously improve whatever we do.

Vision

To innovate, design and build sustainable, efficient, cost effective solutions to improve living conditions of all the life on earth and beyond.
